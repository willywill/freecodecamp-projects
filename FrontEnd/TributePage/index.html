Elon Musk

An Engineer that doesn't fear dreaming too big.


Timeline:

  • 1971 - Elon Musk was born in South Africa.
  • 1990 - Elon Musk attended university in Canada.
  • 1995 - Elon and his brother started a company called Zip2
  • 1999 - Elon co-founded x.com after selling Zip2 which later became PayPal.
  • 2003 - Elon joined the board of Tesla, helping to shape the future of electric vehicles.
  • Current - Elon Musk is partly responsible for the electric car boom and SpaceX, the company that will take us to Mars.
